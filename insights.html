Alitigator known for his detail-oriented work ethic and often-creative approach to cases, Mr. VerStandig has argued before state and federal courts hundreds of times, trying numerous cases to verdict and achieving client-favorable settlements on countless others. He has served as lead counsel in a multitude of high profile and precedent-setting cases, regularly presents oral argument to appellate tribunals throughout the United States, and is often called upon to advise other attorneys on their courtroom manner and strategy.

Mr. VerStandig has represented individuals, small businesses, an international charity, a Fortune 300 mainstay, and even a national trade association in litigation matters of all sizes and varieties. He has adeptly managed contract cases, tort claims, injunctive litigation, and declaratory judgment actions, as well as questions of liability and damage apportionment, and through it all he has developed a unique poise and composure that he uses to ably advance his clients' causes in courtrooms the nation over.
