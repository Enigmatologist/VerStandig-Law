Ms. Patterson is a dynamic oral advocate, with a passion for helping people handle complex legal problems. She has experience in trial and appellate work in a variety of legal fields, including criminal, estate administration, and civil litigation. Judges and opposing counsel alike have complimented her courtroom presence, as well as her adept arguments. Following her graduation from Duke University School of Law, Ms. Patterson served as Staff Attorney for the Fairfax Commissioner of Accounts, overseeing estate administration, obtaining judgments against delinquent fiduciaries, and advising well-meaning fiduciaries to an agreeable resolution of their estate. With the Commissioner of Accounts, she spent over two years learning the basics of estate administration, as well as dealing with more unusual estate administration issues.
