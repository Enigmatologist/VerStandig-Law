Molly Anne Mossey joined the VerStandig Law Firm in February of 2017 as the Office Manager and Director of Marketing. She is a graduate of the College of St. Benedict, where she studied communication, peace studies, and fine arts. Ms. Mossey also studied at Bunkyo Gakuin University in Japan, where she focused on marketing, Japanese, and international business.

Background

Ms. Mossey has an extensive background in office and project management which she chiefly acquired over the course of eight years at Brown and Caldwell, an environmental engineering consulting firm. She is also a longtime recreational poker player, and in 2015, made the transition into poker media. She has worked for many of the top brands in the industry, including the World Series of Poker and PokerNews, and is also the exclusive in-house blogger for Foxwoods Resort and Casino. These experiences, combined with her excellent interpersonal communication skills, make Ms. Mossey a uniquely valuable asset the VerStandig team.
