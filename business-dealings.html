Business Dealings

More Practice Areas

Frequently called upon by private equity firms, individual investors, and corporate entities of all varieties to negotiate, facilitate, and close corporate deals, Mr. VerStandig has counseled clients through transactions involving tens of millions of dollars in real estate corporate equity.

Serving as outside general counsel to businesses of various sizes, Mr. VerStandig is adept at managing corporate takeovers, mergers, real estate acquisitions, and all manner of transactions from the nascent assembly of a letter of intent through the application of wet ink to closing papers. He negotiates with equal emphasis on the conservative values that protect his clients and the liberal theories that allow otherwise-unworkable deals to come to fruition through creative lawyering.
